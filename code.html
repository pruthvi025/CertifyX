Workflow
Three steps. That's it.

No accounts, no cloud uploads, no waiting. Everything runs right in your browser.

01

Upload your template

Drop in any certificate image (PNG, JPG) or PDF. CertifyX uses it as the background for every certificate.

02

Upload Excel data

Upload your spreadsheet (.xlsx or .csv) with student names, IDs, dates — any columns you need mapped.

03

Map your fields

Tell CertifyX which Excel column goes where on the certificate. Drag text fields to position them visually.

04

Download your ZIP

Hit generate. Preview the first certificate, then download a ZIP with every certificate named by student.
